Our Standard

We Refuse To Compromise On Quality.

Our BBQ is cooked low and slow — and by slow, we mean it. Our briskets from start to serving take about 24 hours. No matter what, you can be sure you're eating the best BBQ that's made with care, love, and patience.

  • Low and slow, all night

    Briskets go on the pit the day before they hit your plate.

  • Packed to travel well

    We package our meals so the quality makes it all the way home with you.

  • One pit, one location

    Everything is smoked right here at 6062 Holly Road on the Southside.

Our Story

It Started With A Rinky Dink Offset Smoker.

And we burnt the first two briskets. Much has changed since then.

Three years on Ayers

We ran Garrett's at our first location for three years on Ayers St. Then we took a four year break.

A year on Staples

We started back up at 322 N Staples St. for a year — and the line came right back with us.

Now on Holly Road

Today we're at 6062 Holly Rd #B4 on the Southside, bringing you barbecue once again.
